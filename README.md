# Oglasino Platform

Public home for platform-wide reference documents for Oglasino, an online classifieds platform serving Serbia and Montenegro. The repository currently hosts legal documents pending lawyer review; over time it will hold any other platform-wide reference material that benefits from being publicly readable.

## What Oglasino is

Oglasino is an online classifieds platform for buying, selling, and exchanging new and used goods between private users. It operates two country portals — Serbia (rs) and Montenegro (me) — and is accessible to visitors from other countries, including EU member states. Oglasino does not handle or mediate payments; transactions are arranged and completed directly between users.

The platform is operated as a sole proprietorship by Igor Stojanović, based in Niš, Serbia.
